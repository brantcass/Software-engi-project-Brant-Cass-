Large Mouth Bass


Fly patterns that work really well on largemouth are clouser minnows, poppers(frogs), woolly bugger, Crayfish patters.

Small Mouth Bass


Small mouth are very aggressive so they love a lot of commotion in the water like frog poppers, grasshoppers, dahlberg diver, and during cicada hatches cicada imitations are an excellent choice.

Blue gill


Same with the bass, blue gill love poppers, you will have no problem catching one with a smaller frog popper. Make sure the hook is a bit smaller since blue gill are small fish. They love nymphs, foam bugs and ant patterns. These fish love smaller little bigs the best since they have such small mouths.
