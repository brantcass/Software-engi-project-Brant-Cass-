Fly Casting Motion











Relaxed Grip

Hold the fly rod with a relaxed grip, putting your thumb on top and fingers below. Do not squeeze the rod. Practice is key, focusing on your wrist rather than your arm movment is key. A slow motion of flicking your wrist back and forth to get a whip like motion on your line. Alt text: The image is showing a casting motion of flicking it back and forth to gain power.

Aiming fly line











Aiming for a target

Aiming your fly line is a little bit harder than casting it. Once you get a good cast down, you want to make sure the motion of the fly line is going in a nice loop like in the visual above. This will allow you to use your pole to aim properly. If the line is not in a loop like the visual or is tangled up then it will go to the side of you or behind you.
